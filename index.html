ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Mrs. Arnold! I’ve always had a deep passion for cooking and believe that food is a powerful way to bring people together. Throughout my life, I’ve enjoyed working with people of all ages, which has shaped my personal and professional journey. In high school, my food classes were always my favorite part of the day—they made me excited to go to school, and it was through those experiences that I realized my dream of becoming a Family and Consumer Science teacher.
